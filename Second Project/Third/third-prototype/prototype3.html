THE ARTS.



Parsons hosts its first Parley Talk on April 19, as part of The New School's Earth Week events series. This talk features guest speakers TBD and explores the role of artists, fashion designers, filmmakers, and product designers in reshaping the current reality of our oceans.



PARLEY?

Parley for the Oceans addresses major threats towards our oceans, the most important ecosystem of our planet. Parley believes the power for change lies in the hands of the consumer when given a choice, and the power to shape this new consumer mindset lies in the hands of the creative industries.

Having the tools to mold the reality we live in and to develop alternative business models and ecologically sensible products to give us earthlings an alternative choice, an everyday option to change something.

Parley is the space where creators, thinkers and leaders come together to raise awareness for the beauty and fragility of our oceans and collaborate on projects that can end their destruction.
